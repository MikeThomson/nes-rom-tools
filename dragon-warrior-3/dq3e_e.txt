* What this is
  This is the enemies viewer of DragonQuest3/DragonWarrior3.
  You need the DQ3 or DW3 ROM. But, don't ask me about ROM. 

* Keys

  - Arrow        change enemy
  - Enter        view the last compressed block ON/OFF
  - Space        view glid ON/OFF, and SPR/BG
  - 1            block view -
  - 2            block view +

    * Because I do not know the text data of DW3, 
      this does not display the text (enemies name/item). 

    * "Small Fonts" included in Windows is necessary
      for the display of block information and status.
